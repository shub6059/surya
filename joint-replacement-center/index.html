Why Consult at Surya Hospitals?

Surya Hospitals is one of the leading and most advanced healthcare brands in India. At Surya’s Joint Replacement Center, we have one goal - to help our patients restore the same quality of life they enjoyed before the beginning of pain and discomfort. Our team of superspecialists and support staff at Surya Hospitals provide world class care with individualized attention on progress of the patients.

Joint Replacement Center Specialities

Our Joint Replacement Center is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, highly-trained support staff, and immensely experienced orthopaedic surgeons and doctors who are equipped to treat a wide range of joint and bone ailments.

We employ minimally invasive surgeries that offer the best treatment for joint replacements. Our procedures significantly reduce the pain and time required for recuperation and ensure a shorter hospital stay. Our patients receive a holistic approach & specialized personal care, with various personalized healthcare packages crafted with quality patient care & comfort in mind.

Our Joint Replacement Centre offers the following services:

  • Hip Arthroscopy
  • Hip Replacement
  • Shoulder Surgery
  • Partial Knee Replacement
  • Total Knee Replacement
  • Arthroscopy
  • Bone Tumor Treatment
  • Hip Implants
