Children’s GIT Conditions Treatment Offered


Children of all age group if suffering from any of the below mentioned symptoms and acute/chronic conditions should get in touch immediately -

  • Jaundice in new born
  • Kids complaining of acidity, gases/flatulence, stomach ache, vomiting
  • Loss of appetite
  • Not gaining weight or height as compared to other children of the same age
  • Vomiting of blood, swollen or distended abdomen, fluid in abdomen/ascites
  • Various food allergies – Wheat (Celiac), Cow’s milk (CMPA), etc.
  • Emergencies such as – ingestion of foreign objects – coins, pins, battery cells, etc.
  • Diagnosis, vaccination and management of Hepatitis A, B, C and E
  • Grave illness or affections of Liver, Gall Bladder, Spleen and Pancreas
  • T.B. (Tuberculosis/Koch) of Intestines

Expert & Advanced Surgical GIT Procedures


Following are few of the advanced diagnostic and therapeutic procedures related to gastro-intestinal system performed at Surya Hospitals, Jaipur

  • Endoscopy – Passing of scope via the mouth to diagnose conditions of esophagus, stomach and small intestine
  • Colonoscopy – investigation and diagnosis of conditions related to the large intestine. The scope here is passed via the anal opening
  • Liver Biopsy – A painless procedure with the use of latest technology
  • Procedures to treat the conditions of Esophagus
  • Dilation of the strictures in esophagus
  • Excision or removal of polyps of large intestine
