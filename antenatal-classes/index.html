Why Great Expectations Antenatal Classes?

Great Expectations Antenatal Classes are aimed to prepare expecting couples physically, mentally, and emotionally for the journey of pregnancy and parenthood. At Surya Hospitals, our antenatal classes are conducted by Dr. Vibhuti Kanakia who specialize in pre-and-post-natal care. Pre-pregnancy classes will teach you many useful techniques and equip you with information that will help you understand your baby better.

Monitoring Pregnancy

This class tells you what you need to know about monitoring your pregnancy. Periodic monitoring can increase the chances of a normal pregnancy and treat any complications early-on.

Water Birthing

Some women choose to give birth in a tub of warm water as it is believed to reduce the stress of labour on the mother as well as the baby. This class familiarizes you with the process.

Pregnancy Screening

Pregnancy screening tests can help detect any serious health conditions your baby might have. This class will list the different tests to be done in different trimesters of your pregnancy.

Garbh Sanskar

Scientifically-backed Garbh Sanskar proclaims that a mother’s thoughts during pregnancy impact the unborn baby's growth. This class helps you develop habits that will aid your child’s development.

Dermatology

Pregnancy hormones can cause many changes in your body that may lead to skin problems. This class will prepare you for these changes and teach you how to deal with them.

Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy class can relieve some discomforts like back pain commonly experienced by pregnant women. It also helps your body prepare for a smooth pregnancy and birth.

Breathing Exercises

This class will teach you some breathing exercises that will make you feel calm during labour and increase your oxygen intake to increase your strength and energy during childbirth.

Prenatal Yoga

Prenatal yoga class offers many benefits like improving sleep, reducing stress and anxiety, increasing flexibility and muscle endurance, and more.

Therapy

Music meditation during pregnancy is a wonderful stress buster. It can help you relax, stay happy, and feel peaceful.

Labour Management

Labour can be an extremely painful experience while giving birth. Labour management class teaches you some ways to manage the pain naturally.

Nutrition During Pregnancy

During pregnancy, your body needs extra nutrients to meet the needs of your growing baby. Learn which foods to eat to keep your body healthy and strong during this crucial period of your life.

Lactation

Having a new addition to the family can be overwhelming. In this class, you will learn some valuable tips on lactation, breastfeeding, and how to take care of your newborn.

  • One hour sessions every Wednesday and Saturday(6 pm - 7 pm)
  • For participation, mothers must be in between the 2nd and 3rd trimester
  • Sessions must be taken for a minimum of 2 months.
    Mothers can continue the workouts taught in the sessions after the class if health permits
