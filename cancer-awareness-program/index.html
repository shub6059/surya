Why Get Tested for Cancer ?

Every year Mother's Day is held on the second Sunday of May. On this occasion, Surya Hospitals, Pune have started a cancer screening camp to help women detect cancer early and get the treatment needed as soon as possible.
According to the World Health Organization, early cancer detection can greatly increase the chances of successful treatments. When cancer is found early, it can be treated before it has a chance to grow and spread.

How Are Cancers Detected ?

Surya Hospitals are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and super specialists who have decades of experience in diagnosing and treating cancers. As part of this camp we offer:

  • CBC
  • Mammography/Mamogram
  • PAP Smear
  • Complete Physical Examination
  • Consultation by Specialist

These tests and consultation will help us detect any early warning signs of cancer and start treatment, if necessary.
